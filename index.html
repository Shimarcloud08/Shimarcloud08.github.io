Who we are

Travel isn't a destination.
It's a way of seeing.

Fernweh & Files is a travel brand built on the belief that the best journeys are the ones that leave you changed. We don't book itineraries — we curate experiences rooted in local culture, slow travel, and genuine human connection.

Every destination we feature has been lived in, not just visited. Every story we tell has a real person at its centre. Every trip we plan is designed around what you want to feel, not just what you want to see.

What we believe in

Culture is not a backdrop. It's the whole story.

Too much travel writing treats culture as scenery — the colourful costumes you photograph before getting back on the bus. We think that's the saddest thing you can do with a passport.

Every story here asks what people believe, how they celebrate, what they eat when the tourists aren't watching — and why any of it matters to the rest of the world.
